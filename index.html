About

I'm a Data Scientist at ITSC GmbH, building AI solutions for the public health insurance sector. I'm passionate about integrating ML models into traditional workflows — supporting and enhancing the work people already do, not replacing it. My goal is to help people live longer, healthier lives by bringing data-driven insights into the processes that matter.

Before moving into industry, I spent five years as a researcher at Heinz Nixdorf Institute, where I earned my Ph.D. in theoretical computer science and published 13 peer-reviewed papers on distributed algorithms and robot coordination. That academic background gives me a strong foundation for breaking down complex problems and designing rigorous solutions — skills I apply daily when building scalable ML pipelines and RAG-based architectures.
